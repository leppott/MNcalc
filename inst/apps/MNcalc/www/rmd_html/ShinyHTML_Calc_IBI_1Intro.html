Calculation – IBI

The Index of Biotic Integrity (IBI) is one of the primary tools used by the MPCA to determine if streams are meeting their aquatic life use goals. The IBIs are comprised of biological metrics that were found to be responsive to a general stressor gradient. Examples of bug and fish IBI input metrics and scoring formulas can be found in the bug and fish subtabs. The IBI scoring scale ranges from 0 (worst) to 100 (best).

The Shiny app will perform the following steps:

  1. Mark non-distinct taxa (excluded from richness metrics)

  2. Calculate metric values for each sample

  3. Calculate metric scores for each sample

  4. Calculate overall IBI score for each sample
